Greetings! I am currently a Assistant Professor with the School of Computer at the National University of Defense Technology (NUDT). I received my B.E. degree from College of Information Engineering, Nanchang University in 2010, my M.S. and Ph.D.degrees both supervised by Prof. Zhigang Luo from School of Computer, at the National University of Defense Technology in 2012 and 2017, respectively. I was a two-year (2015-2016) visting student with the Centre for Quantum Computation and Intelligent Systems (QCIS), at the University of Technology, Sdyney (UTS) supervised by Prof. Dacheng Tao.

Research Interests

My research interests mainly lie in designing efficient learning algorithms for real world computer vision tasks

  • Visual Object Detection and Tracking (Multi-object Tracking, Video Object Detection)

  • Person Re-identification

  • Semi-supervised Learning (Non-negative Matrix Factorization)
