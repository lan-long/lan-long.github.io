Greetings! I am an Associate Professor with the School of Computer, at the National University of Defense Technology (NUDT). I received my Ph. D. degree from the NUDT in 2017 supervised by Prof. Zhigang Luo. I was a two-year (2015-2016) visiting student with the Centre for Quantum Computation and Intelligent Systems (QCIS), at the University of Technology, Sydney (UTS) supervised by Prof. Dacheng Tao.

Research Interests

My research interests mainly lie in designing efficient learning algorithms for real-world computer vision tasks

  • Visual Object Detection and Tracking (Multi-object Tracking, Video Object Detection)

  • Person Re-identification

  • Few Shot learning (Transfer learning)
