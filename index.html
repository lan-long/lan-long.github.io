Research Interests

My research interests mainly lie in designing efficient learning algorithms for real-world computer vision tasks

  • Visual Object Detection and Tracking (Multi-object Tracking, Video Object Detection)

  • Person Re-identification

  • Few Shot learning (Transfer Learning)
